Kross.money is a high performance, web based trading terminal that unifies global decentralized perpetual markets by using account abstraction (via Privy) to offer a gasless, one click Web2 user experience with the absolute security of self custodial finance.

The problem space addresses retail traders who face severe counterparty risks, account freezes, and platform bankruptcies on traditional centralized exchanges (CEXs), yet find existing decentralized finance (DeFi) alternatives structurally confusing and plagued by gas fee friction. The market potential is immense, unlocking institutional grade, non custodial trading for millions of mainstream retail and algorithmic investors globally without compromising their capital control. 

I built Kross.money because active traders are currently forced to compromise between security, speed, and complex Web3 jargon. My goal is to deliver the familiar, seamless execution of a traditional brokerage interface backed by the absolute transparency and safety of a complete DeFi architecture.
