Slide 2 — Problem & Solution
The Opportunity Kross.money 02 / 06
The market is broken.
⚠
The Problem with Today's Options
  • Centralized exchanges control user funds - they can lend, invest, or freeze them.
  • DeFi platforms require complex setup like wallets, seed phrases, gas fee management.
  • Most retail traders quit before they ever place a trade on crypto exchanges.
  • Self-custody on a DEX has always meant slower execution.
✦
The Kross.money resolution
  • Sign in with Google or Apple ID - no wallet setup, no seed phrases required.
  • Non-custodial smart wallets via account abstraction - technically impossible for us to touch user funds.
  • Gas, abstracted away. The UX feels like a CEX but the custody doesn't.
  • All trades settle permissionlessly, always-on, no single point of failure.
